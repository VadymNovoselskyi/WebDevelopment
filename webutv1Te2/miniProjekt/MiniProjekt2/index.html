Introduction

This website is dedicated to rating of mostly western food such as English breakfast, cereals with milk, pizza, hamburgers, beef stew, tiramisu, bcolnies and much more. But there is also featured food that is not as common in western countries, such as borscht kharcho, baklava and more.


How the food was rated

The food is rated based on my own subjective preferences and experience, so keep that in my mind.
